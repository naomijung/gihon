Gihon Women Empowerment Kenya aims to transform the lives of women living in poverty, survivors of domestic abuse, and other disadvantaged women by empowering them to lead independent, prosperous, and happy lives.

We also engage in several other community projects, including childcare and HIV support groups, to foster our Kitengela community.
